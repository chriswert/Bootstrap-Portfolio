About Me

Hi, my name is Christopher Werther but you can call me Chris. I am the son of a retired, 30 year Chief Master Sargent of the U.S. Air Force. Being a military brat, I have lived in many places. I was born in Arizona at Luke Air Force Base, we then moved to Kent Ohio. Our next assignment was to the Air Force Acadamy where my dad worked at the infamous NORAD Space Command installation. We then moved to Naples Italy, Gosnell Arkansas and finaly Ramstein Germany. After graduating from High School in Germany, I took a year to travel europe. In 1995, began college at the University of Arizona. There I met my wife of now 20 years, Christina. After transfering the Northern Arizona University's Online campus and began to finish my Bachelor's Degree while my wife attended Law School at Gonzaga University in Spokane Washington, It is here I decided to explore my pasion in golf.
When we moved back to Phoenix, I got a job at a golf course where I quickly learned the day to day operations. I soon began to teach golf and I even passed the PAT test to become a PGA Apprentice.I started to study the buiness adminstration part of the PGA. After 3 years and some life events, I decided that this was not the path I truly wanted to take. After a year away, one of my former Head Golf Pro's called me and asked if I would be interested in working for him at a Golf Management company overseeing the companies courses with their Email Marketing, Website Support and Revenue Management. I took the chance and said yes. The job was great! I learned so much in a short amount of time and I soon realized I really enjoyed the website design and coding of pages. After a year and half with the company, I decided that I wanted to become more proficient in website design and coding. I am now attending school to become a Full Stack Web Developer. My hope is to bring great skill and my passion for golf and begin creating spectacular golf course website designs!
